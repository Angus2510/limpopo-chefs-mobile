Privacy Policy - Limpopo Chefs Academy Mobile App

Last Updated: November 6, 2025

Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Student credentials (username/password) for authentication
  • Student profile information (name, ID, contact details)
  • Academic records and attendance data
  • Fee and payment information

Usage Information

  • App usage statistics
  • Device information (iOS/Android version, device model)
  • Login timestamps and session data

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide access to student portal features
  • Display academic progress and attendance
  • Send important announcements and updates
  • Process fee payments and transactions
  • Improve app functionality and user experience

Data Security

  • All data is transmitted using secure HTTPS encryption
  • Student credentials are securely stored using industry-standard practices
  • Access is restricted to authenticated students only
  • Regular security audits and updates

Data Sharing

We do NOT:

  • Sell your personal information to third parties
  • Share your data with external marketing companies
  • Use your information for advertising purposes

We only share information:

  • With Limpopo Chefs Academy internal systems
  • When required by law or educational regulations
  • With your explicit consent

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Request corrections to your data
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing

Contact Us

For privacy concerns or questions:

  • Email: privacy@limpopochefs.co.za
  • Website: portal.limpopochefs.co.za

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Students will be notified of any significant changes through the app or academy communications.

By using the Limpopo Chefs Academy mobile app, you agree to this privacy policy.
